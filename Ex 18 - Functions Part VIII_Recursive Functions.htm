Building a Recursive Function

Recursion is a well-known concept in the fields of Mathematics and Computer Science. Two famous examples of using Recursive Algorithms are the Fibonacci Sequence of numbers and the computation of a number's Factorial.

Essentially a Recursive Algorithm is an algorithm that in every step computes a simpler invocation of itself, down to a simple constant that we know.
